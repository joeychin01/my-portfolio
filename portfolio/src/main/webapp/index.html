About me

My name is Joseph Chinoski, and I am a rising junior at The University of Michigan (Go Blue!). At UMich, I am a Computer Science Major in the College of Engineering. Besides coding, I play tennis, read books (especially Stephen King), and play the violin!

Experiences

Over the summer in 2019, I participated in CSSI, a Google coding camp that is intended for incoming College freshmen to learn what it is like to be a software developer, as well as to learn some coding skills revolving around web development.

Projects
